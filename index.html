Pac-Man. Free. Runs in the browser.

It is Pac-Man. The maze, the ghosts, the pellets. 1980 Namco rules, running in a browser tab, no account required. Three modes: play it yourself, watch the AI play it, or take control of one of the ghosts while the AI runs Pac-Man.

Watch AI

The AI plays Pac-Man. Before it starts you pick a personality. Coward avoids ghosts compulsively, even at the cost of pellets. Balanced is competent and boring. Aggressive hunts ghosts after every energizer, which works until it does not. Greedy ignores the ghosts for pellets, and survives longer than it has any right to.

The ghosts follow classic Namco behavior with minor randomization in release timing, scatter phases and pathfinding. No two games are identical.

Leaderboard

Global leaderboard. Scores are HMAC-signed server-side before posting, so the numbers are real. Pick a username, play, your best score submits at the end. No account.
